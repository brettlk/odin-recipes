Lasagna

Indulge in our rich and comforting Classic Lasagna, a beloved Italian favorite that layers flavor and texture in every bite. This hearty dish features sheets of tender pasta enveloped in a velvety blend of seasoned ground beef and pork, simmered to perfection in a robust marinara sauce. We add creamy ricotta and fresh mozzarella for that perfect melt, while a sprinkle of Parmesan adds a touch of savory depth.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Ground pork
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Italian seasoning
  • Sea salt
  • Rock pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and sauté until translucent.
  3. Add ground beef and ground pork to the skillet. Cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon.
  4. Stir in marinara sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with a pinch of salt and freshly chopped basil.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Layer lasagna sheets over the sauce, followed by a layer of the ricotta mixture, then mozzarella cheese, and more meat sauce.
