Orange Slice

Delight in our refreshing Orange Slices, a simple yet vibrant treat that captures the essence of citrus in every bite. These juicy slices are perfect as a light snack, a colorful addition to any dessert platter, or a zesty garnish for your favorite dishes.

Ingredients

  • Fresh oranges
  • Honey (optional)
  • Cinnamon (optional)
  • Mint leaves (for garnish, optional)

Steps

  1. Begin by washing the oranges thoroughly under cold water.
  2. Using a sharp knife, slice the oranges into thin rounds, about 1/4 inch thick.
  3. Remove any seeds from the slices, if necessary.
  4. Arrange the orange slices on a serving platter.
  5. If desired, drizzle with honey for added sweetness and sprinkle with a dash of cinnamon.
  6. Garnish with fresh mint leaves for a pop of color and flavor.
  7. Serve immediately, or refrigerate for a refreshing treat later!
