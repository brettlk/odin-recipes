Chicken Parmigiana

Enjoy our irresistible Chicken Parmigiana, a classic Italian-American dish that brings together crispy breaded chicken cutlets, rich marinara sauce, and gooey melted cheese. Each bite is a delightful combination of savory flavors and textures, perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts (flattened)
  • Flour (for dredging)
  • Eggs (beaten)
  • Breadcrumbs (preferably Italian-style)
  • Marinara sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese (sliced)
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Fresh basil (for garnish)
  • Olive oil (for frying)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Italian seasoning
  • Sea salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Set up a breading station: Place flour in one bowl, beaten eggs in another, and breadcrumbs mixed with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper in a third.
  3. Dredge each chicken breast in flour, dip into the egg, then coat with breadcrumbs, pressing to adhere.
  4. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Fry the breaded chicken cutlets for about 4-5 minutes on each side, until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of marinara sauce. Place the fried chicken cutlets on top.
  6. Top each cutlet with marinara sauce, then layer with fresh mozzarella slices and a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for about 20 minutes, until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  8. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.
